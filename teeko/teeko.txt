Teeko is played on a 5x5 board. there are two players: Blue and Red, each having 4 checkers of the corresponding color
The goal of Teeko is to place four of your own checkers in horizontally, vertically, diagonally or in a 2x2square

The game starts with an empty board. 
The player controlling the blue pieces starts first. Players take turns placing on of their own checkers in any unoccupied spot on the board
If neither player has won after placing all 8 checkers, the players continue the game by moving one of their checkers per turn to an
adjacent unoccupied cell until a player wins

To move pieces after they have been place on the board, the player must click on the piece and then on an empty cell. 

To pause the game or to go to the instructions, press 'p' on the keyboard
